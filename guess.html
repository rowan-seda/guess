How to play Guess

    The goal:
    There is a random sequence of colors that you need to match.
    Each turn:
    Place four colors in the four slots. A color can be used more than once per sequence. Once each slot is filled you will get clues for how accurate your guess is.
    Reading the clues
    For each number of colors that you have correct a slot in the clues section will be filled. Red means you got a color in the correct slot. White means you got a correct color but not in the correct slot. note: The position of the clues does not indicate the position of the correct colors
    You get ten turns to get it right.
    Enjoy
